Shrimp Scampi


Shrimp scampi is a seafood dish made of shrimp cooked in a butter, garlic, and white wine sauce. You can serve shrimp scampi by itself as an appetizer or over pasta as a main dish.



    Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • EV Olive Oil
  • Shallots and Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seasonings
  • Parsley
